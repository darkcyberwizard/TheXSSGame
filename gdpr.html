GDPR Privacy Notice for The XSS Game Players

Effective Date: September 20, 2025

This Privacy Notice explains what information is collected from players of The XSS Game ("you" or "your"), how that information is used, and your rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

1. Information We Collect

The XSS Game does not collect any personal data. Only non-personal gameplay data (such as in-game actions, choices, and interactions) may be gathered for analytical and research purposes. This data cannot be used to identify you.

2. Consent

By playing The XSS Game, you consent to the collection of non-personal data for the purposes of improving the game and understanding gameplay dynamics. Consent is voluntary but required to participate.

3. Withdrawing Consent

You may withdraw your consent at any time by contacting us at nipuna.h.weeratunge@ntnu.no. If you withdraw consent, no further data will be collected from your gameplay.

4. Use of Data

The non-personal data collected is used exclusively for internal analysis and aggregation to better understand player behavior and improve The XSS Game. No data will be shared, sold, or disclosed to third parties unless legally required.

5. Data Security

We implement strong technical and organizational measures to ensure that all collected data is stored securely and protected against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

6. Your Rights

Under the GDPR, you have rights regarding any data collected, including:

  • The right to access: Request a copy of the data held about you.

  • The right to rectification: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.

  • The right to erasure: Request deletion of your data under certain conditions.

  • The right to restrict processing: Request limited use of your data under certain conditions.

  • The right to object: Object to processing under certain conditions.

  • The right to data portability: Request transfer of your data to another organization or to you, under certain conditions.

To exercise these rights, please contact us at the email provided in Section 3. We will respond to your request within one month.

7. Changes to This Privacy Notice

We may update this Privacy Notice from time to time in response to legal, technical, or business changes. If significant updates are made, we will notify you appropriately.

8. Contact Us

For questions or concerns regarding this Privacy Notice or our data practices, please contact us at nipuna.h.weeratunge@ntnu.no.

By continuing to play The XSS Game, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Notice.
